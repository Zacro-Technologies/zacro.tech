Owned by the people

Community stewardship, not company control.

Zacro AI is designed to stay independent. Governance focuses on transparency, anti-capture rules, and simple operations that the community can maintain.

Anti‑capture

One member, one vote. Public reports. Multisig treasury. Open source core.

Who runs it (proposed roles)

Clear responsibilities, minimal bureaucracy.

Members

Vote on budgets, stewards, and major policy changes. One member, one vote.

Steward council

Sets high-level priorities, publishes reports, and coordinates community decisions.

Maintainers

Ship releases, manage security updates, and keep the protocol and client reliable.

Security response

Handles coordinated vulnerability disclosure and incident response.

Treasury signers

Multisig approvals for spending; prevents any single person from moving funds.

Moderation

Abuse prevention, policy enforcement, and a documented appeals process.

How it’s run (day-to-day)

  1. Operate the scheduler and verification services with public uptime/incident reporting.
  2. Maintain the node client with reproducible releases and clear change logs.
  3. Enforce abuse limits so free best-effort access stays usable.
Funding (without paywalls): membership dues + recurring donations + capped sponsorships (no special routing, no control).
