Compute savings

Credits = your saved idle compute.

You don’t run AI all day. Zacro AI turns your device’s idle time into useful work for the network, and records that contribution as credits. Later, you spend those credits to get priority access for big parallel workloads.

Coming Soon Contribute

No cash‑out

Credits are usage rights (non‑transferable). The goal is to replace subscriptions with contribution, not to create “earn money” mechanics.

The simple rules

  1. Contribute verified work while idle → credits are saved.
  2. Spend credits → priority scheduling for parallel workloads.
  3. No contribution? You can still use best‑effort access when idle capacity exists (limited + preemptible).
Fairness: contribution is weighted by recent reliability, so “contribute once, use forever” doesn’t work.
